Natural Language Processing

Natural language processing (NLP) is a subfield of both linguistics and computer science. NLP is primarily concerned with analyzing qualitative data (text) through the use of various techniques, algorithms, and statistical models. This course offers a gentle technical introduction to the world of NLP.

Education

Experience

Mr. Paolucci worked as a quantitative researcher for almost 2 years before pursuing a career in education. He primarily researched the implementation of AI in derivative pricing solutions to speedup pricing computations for models without closed form solutions. He also conducted alpha research using large alternative datasets of hundreds of millions of observations to create and sell algorithmic investing strategies.
